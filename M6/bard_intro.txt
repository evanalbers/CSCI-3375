Welcome to Bard, a computationally creative poetry generator! Bard will
generate poems at your request, and even read them aloud to you. 

List of commands:

 - generate (g) -> Bard generates a new limerick
 - play (p) -> Bard reads the most recently generated limerick out loud
 - train bard (tb [generations] [models per generation]) -> Train Bard starting at the most recent generation
 - quit (q) -> quits program
